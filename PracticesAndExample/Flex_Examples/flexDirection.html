Key Takeaways for flex-direction:

  • row: Default value, aligns items horizontally from left to right.
  • row-reverse: Aligns items horizontally from right to left.
  • column: Aligns items vertically from top to bottom.
  • column-reverse: Aligns items vertically from bottom to top.

Best Practice: Use row for horizontal layouts and column for vertical layouts. Reverse values are useful for specific UI needs like right-aligned menus or reverse stacking.
