Key Takeaways for justify-content:

  • flex-start: Aligns items to the start of the main axis.
  • flex-end: Aligns items to the end of the main axis.
  • center: Aligns items to the center of the main axis.
  • space-between: Distributes items with maximum space between.
  • space-around: Distributes items with space around each.
  • space-evenly: Distributes items with equal space between.

Best Practice: Use flex-start for default layouts and space-between for equal distribution.
