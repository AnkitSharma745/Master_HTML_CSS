Key Takeaways for flex-wrap:

  • nowrap: Default value, all items are placed on a single line, even if they overflow.
  • wrap: Items wrap onto new lines when needed, from top to bottom.
  • wrap-reverse: Items wrap onto new lines when needed, but from bottom to top.

Best Practice: Use nowrap for simple, single-line layouts. Wrap is ideal for responsive designs, and wrap-reverse is suitable for specialized layouts.
