Key Takeaways for align-content:

  • flex-start: Rows are packed at the top.
  • flex-end: Rows are packed at the bottom.
  • center: Rows are centered in the container.
  • space-between: Equal space between rows.
  • space-around: Equal space around rows.
  • space-evenly: Equal space between, before, and after rows.

Best Practice: Use align-content to control row alignment in multi-line flex containers. Choose a value that enhances the visual balance of your layout.
