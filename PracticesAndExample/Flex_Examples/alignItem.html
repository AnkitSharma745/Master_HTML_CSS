Key Takeaways for align-items:

  • flex-start: Aligns items to the start of the cross axis.
  • flex-end: Aligns items to the end of the cross axis.
  • center: Aligns items to the center of the cross axis.
  • stretch: Stretches items to fill the container's cross axis.
  • baseline: Aligns items based on their text baseline.

Best Practice: Use align-items: center for vertical centering and stretch for uniform alignment.
