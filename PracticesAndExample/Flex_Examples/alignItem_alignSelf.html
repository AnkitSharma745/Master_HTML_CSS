align-items vs align-self

1. align-items (Aligning All Items)

The align-items property is used to align all flex items along the cross axis (vertical by default). It affects every item within the container.

2. align-self (Aligning Individual Items)

The align-self property is used to align an individual item along the cross axis, overriding the align-items setting for that item.

Key Takeaways:

  • align-items: Aligns all items along the cross axis in the container.
  • align-self: Aligns an individual item along the cross axis, overriding the container's align-items value.
  • Use align-items to control the alignment of all items, and align-self to override the default alignment for specific items.

Best Practice: Use align-items for consistent alignment across items and align-self when you need to adjust the position of a single item.
